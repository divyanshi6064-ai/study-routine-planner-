##### Smart Daily Routine Optimizer







### 2\. Problem Statement



Students often struggle with planning their daily activities such as study hours, class hours, sleep, eating time, refresh time, and hobby time. Due to poor routine structuring, productivity decreases and academic performance is affected.  

This project aims to generate a balanced and optimized daily routine based on simple user inputs.







### 3\. Scope of the Project



###### The scope of this project includes:



\- Collecting daily time-related inputs from the user

\- Splitting study sessions into morning and evening

\- Dividing eating hours into breakfast, lunch, and dinner

\- Calculating productivity score

\- Generating a clean, formatted routine table

\- Providing suggestion based on the score

\- Displaying current month calendar

\- Showing digital and analog clocks

\- Providing a focus timer to help maintain discipline



###### The project focuses on applying fundamentals of problem solving, modular programming, loops, conditionals, arithmetic calculations, and GUI design using Tkinter.
